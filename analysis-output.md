### Pearson Correlation (cm/360 vs accuracy, weighted by bracket midpoint)

**Railgun:** r = 0.854 — very strong positive correlation. accuracy tends to increase with lower sensitivity (higher cm/360).

**Lightning Gun:** r = 0.715 — strong positive correlation. accuracy tends to increase with lower sensitivity (higher cm/360).

**Rocket Launcher:** r = 0.041 — very weak positive correlation. accuracy tends to increase with lower sensitivity (higher cm/360).

> Note: Positive r means accuracy goes up as cm/360 increases (lower sens = better accuracy).
> Negative r means accuracy goes up as cm/360 decreases (higher sens = better accuracy).
> Correlation does not imply causation — player skill, playstyle, and game mode all affect accuracy.
